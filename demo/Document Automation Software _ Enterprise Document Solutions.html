ActiveDocs is an enterprise platform that makes complex documentation simple. Our document automation software creates, processes, and manages your most critical documents with AI-powered precision and control, helping every project move forward with confidence.

Effortless AI Document Generation Software & Automation Solutions

ActiveDocs was founded more than 3 decades ago to help regulated industries remove the friction from documentation. From our origins in New Zealand, we have grown into a trusted global solution for governments, financial institutions, and healthcare organizations.

Today, we bring together AI-enabled document generation, intelligent templates, and a powerful rules engine, built directly inside Microsoft Word, to create document processing solutions that are fast, compliant, and governed. Our deeply integrated and scalable architecture makes it effortless for enterprises to move from draft to delivery with speed and precision.

As one of the leading document automation providers globally, ActiveDocs serves high-security sectors, including banking, insurance, finance, and federal governments.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is document automation software?

+
-

Our software automates the entire lifecycle of your business documents. Using templates and rules-based logic, it integrates directly with your data to streamline distribution.

It eliminates manual document creation, reduces errors, ensures consistency, and accelerates document production at scale. ActiveDocs is enterprise-grade document generation software, designed for high-volume, compliance-critical environments.

How does ActiveDocs integrate with Microsoft Word?

+
-

ActiveDocs is built directly inside Microsoft Word, allowing business users to design and maintain templates using familiar tools. There’s no proprietary software to learn—your team can create intelligent templates, apply conditional logic, and incorporate data sources all within the Word environment they already know.

This approach significantly reduces training time and empowers template authors to make updates independently of IT.

What industries does ActiveDocs serve?

+
-

ActiveDocs serves high-security and highly regulated industries, including banking, insurance, finance, healthcare, legal, and more. Our document processing solutions are designed for organizations that require accuracy, compliance, and audit trails in their document processes.

How long does it take to implement ActiveDocs?

+
-

Implementation timelines vary based on the complexity of your document processes and integration requirements. ActiveDocs offers a Proof of Concept approach that allows you to trial the software in your own environment before full deployment.

Our implementation services team provides support throughout the process, and our track record of customers partnering with us for 18-19 years demonstrates our commitment to long-term success.
